weatherlearn.PTL.grant_baselines module¶

Compute dataset statistics + persistence / climatology baseline metrics on the preprocessed SuperDARN cnvmap pairs. No model training needed.

Usage:

python grant_baselines.py [–data_dir ~/rst/preprocessed/g120_f500]
